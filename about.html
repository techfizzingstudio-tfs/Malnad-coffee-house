OUR STORY

Born in Chikmagalur.

Malnad Coffee House was not built in a boardroom. It was built with a deep understanding of the land — the misty, shade-grown coffee estates of Chikmagalur, Karnataka, where some of India's finest Arabica and Robusta has been grown for over two centuries.

We are a unit of MNZ Ventures Pvt. Ltd., a Chikmagalur-based business group. Our proximity to the growing region is not a marketing point — it is our operational advantage. We source directly from estates, we know the harvest cycles, and we understand the quality differences that elevation, shade, and processing method create.

OUR MISSION

Connecting Indian Coffee to the World

India grows some of the finest coffee in Asia. Yet Indian green beans remain underrepresented in the global specialty market. Our mission is to change that — by connecting serious international buyers with verified, traceable, grade-certified beans from the estates of Chikmagalur and beyond.

Domestically, we aim to be the most reliable B2B coffee supply partner for HoReCa businesses and roasteries across India.

  • Estate-direct sourcing
  • Consistent roasting standards
  • Scalable B2B supply chain
