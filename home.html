OUR STORY

Heritage roasted into every bean we ship.

Born in the misty hills of Chikmagalur — India's coffee heartland — Malnad Coffee House™ is a unit of MNZ Ventures Pvt Ltd. We partner directly with estate farmers, roast in small consistent batches, and supply at scale to cafés, QSR chains, and private labels.

"Chikmagalur has fed the world's coffee for over 200 years. We are simply its most dedicated custodians." — Team Malnad Coffee House
