GRADE REFERENCE TABLE

Coffee Bean Grades — Quick Reference

AAA

Finest Specialty Grade

Superior screen size, minimal defects and premium cup quality.

AA

High Grade

Consistent bean size, clean cup and ideal for specialty roasters.

A

Standard Grade

Good quality for commercial blends and espresso roasts.

B

Mid-Commercial Grade

Suitable for high-volume filter coffee blends.

C

Commercial Grade

Economical option for mass-market blends.

PB

Peaberry

Single-bean cherry prized for intense, concentrated flavour.

AB

Mixed Screen Grade

Versatile and popular for domestic coffee blends.

Bulk

Commercial Volume Supply

Ideal for manufacturers and large-scale processors.
